BOSS! Is a leisure game. In the game, you only have one target, is to kill the BOSS.

You can click the left mouse button to hit BOSS and get money, money can let you leave up to add the damage.

Friends: damage is too low? But the price for leave up is too much? Let’ recruit friends!

Friends can help you hit boss and get money themselves!

Equipment: Still feel too weak? No problem, go to buy some equipment!

It will bring you a variety of bonuses, like:

- Increase damage for each click.

- Increase damage from friend.

- Reduce price for leave up yourself.

- Reduce price for leave up friends.

- Reduce price for buy equipment.
